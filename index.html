About Me

My name is Eva Manabat, and I recently graduated from the University of Michigan with a B.S.E. in Computer Engineering. Go Blue!

I'm passionate about building customer-facing software that people use every day, especially products that make technology more intuitive, accessible, and enjoyable. From robotics to autonomous flight systems to consumer software, my experiences have reinforced my passion for turning ideas into products that people can actually use. I love working with people who bring different perspectives, because the best ideas come from building together.
